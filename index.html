Our Story

BETA-TECH LABS was founded by a team of four driven students who first came together during AI FEST 2025. Collaborating for a hackathon, we developed an AI-powered pest and disease detector.

Inspired by that success and united by a shared vision, we decided to merge our diverse skills and talents to build innovative technology products that will make a global impact.

We are a committed, supportive team of bright minds focused on shaping a brighter future through technology. What keeps us moving forward is our passion for continuous learning and the acceptance that growth comes from embracing new knowledge every day.

Our ambition is sky-high — we're aiming for the moon, but even if we don't reach it, we know we will still land among the stars.
